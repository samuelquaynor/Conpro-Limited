Structural Engineering

Our engineering professionals employ state of art technology in structures, thus, we have the edge in terms of provision large and detailed body of knowledge that predict the performance of different shapes and materials used in structures to resist loads and stresses on structures. Civil engineering defines the urban landscape, creating the sustainable, effective infrastructure that is fundamental to everyday life. There are broad range of civil engineering capabilities which we use to create powerful, bespoke solutions for a diverse range of clients. Our structural engineers are chartered professionals who are good standing with engineering professional bodies always exceeding end user needs so that the structures we use in our daily lives, like bridges and tall buildings, are safe, stable and resilient against all loads. This is achieved by applying technical knowledge to specify different types of construction materials in various shapes and geometries and design structures that can withstand the pressures and stresses of their environment such as gravity loads, storms and earthquakes. Depending on the function of the building, we implore the use of standards, building codes, and sophisticated and up -to- date engineering software to analyze the different kinds of structures. Our structural engineers have it a heart to ensure safety of the public is their top priority. To that effect our structural engineers use factors of safety in their design to account for uncertainty in load assumptions and unexpected deficiency in construction material or workmanship.
